Program Introduction

Gathering Wind - Sending Sunshine is a charity program organized by students of the University of Economics in Ho Chi Minh City. The program includes a series of fundraising activities lasting for one month, aiming to bring material and spiritual values to students and schools.

Vision

We hope the program will become a prestigious annual charity activity, spreading profound humanitarian values and promoting the spirit of sharing within the student community.

Not only material contributions, we hope to inspire and light up dreams for the younger generation.

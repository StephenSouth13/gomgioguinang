Program Introduction

Gather Wind - Send Sunshine is a charity program organized by students from Ho Chi Minh City University of Economics. The program includes a series of fundraising activities lasting for 1 month, aimed at providing material and spiritual value for students and schools.

Vision

We hope that the program will become a reputable and annual charity activity, spreading profound humanitarian values and promoting the spirit of sharing within the student community.

Not only material contributions, we hope to provide motivation and ignite dreams for the younger generation.
